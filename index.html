Table for Ten is a monthly brunch gathering in Central London, focused on creating genuine human connections through meaningful conversations.

How it works

Table for Ten brunches are invitation-only, and I curate the group each month to ensure that it is both diverse and connected by common interests. All I ask of you is to come with an open mind and be yourself.

To get an invitation, simply leave your details in the form at the bottom of the page.

What Table for Ten is not a party, a networking event, or a dating event. It is a safe space to enjoy interesting conversations with new people.
Where Brunches usually take place in a quiet cafe with coffee and food, where we can hear each other and conversations can flow easily.
You We welcome all people who are interested in meeting others and having open conversations.
Our past attendees include entrepreneurs, artists, developers, and more.
Me Table for Ten is organised by me, Alice Lieutier, a 35-year-old French resident of London since 2012. I am passionate about people, education, and adventure.
